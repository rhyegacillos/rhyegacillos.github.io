About

I am a software engineer dedicated to building AI applications where the model is treated as a single component within a larger, reliable system. My focus is on implementing rigorous AI model output verification and truth-gated workflows to ensure that AI behavior remains verifiable and trustworthy.

I specialize in designing asynchronous, serverless architectures that prioritize background execution and clear progress feedback for complex, tool-heavy tasks. I aim to contribute to high-stakes environments that require server side rules for deterministic behavior and a disciplined approach to failure handling. My goal is to deliver end-to-end solutions that are technically robust, operationally transparent, and easy for stakeholders to navigate.
